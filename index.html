Pardis means “paradise” (بهشت) in Persian. We’ve created a space where Iranian-heritage children and families can discover connect and build friendships. Through classes, workshops, events, and learning materials we teach Persian language and Iranian culture while helping families build community.

Pardis for Children is a NYC-based nonprofit that teaches Persian language and culture to children of Iranian heritage through art and music.
